Gemini 3.5: Fluid intelligence at the speed of thought

Six months ago, with the release of Gemini 3.0, we introduced "Deep Think"—a capability allowing models to pause, verify hypotheses, and reason through problems before responding. It was a necessary step for solving the impossible, but it introduced a new friction: the wait.

Today, we are collapsing the distinction between thinking and acting.

We are thrilled to introduce Gemini 3.5. By utilizing a novel architecture we call "Parallelized Latent Verification," Gemini 3.5 maintains the profound reasoning depth of the 3.0 series but operates at the speed of conversation. It achieves a "Flow State," where verification happens simultaneously with generation.

Setting a new standard for intelligence

Gemini 3.5 is not just faster; it is smarter. We have seen the largest jump in "hard" benchmarks since the transition from Gemini 1.0 to 1.5.

On Humanity’s Last Exam (HLE), the benchmark designed to withstand AI capabilities until 2027, Gemini 3.5 has become the first model to cross the 65% threshold, solving graduate-level problems that require novel insight, not just retrieval.

Native Actions & Liquid Memory

Intelligence is of limited use if it remains trapped in a chat box. Gemini 3.5 introduces Native Action capabilities. Instead of outputting code for a human to copy-paste, Gemini 3.5 can now directly interface with secure sandboxed environments to execute complex workflows—from editing video files to compiling apps—autonomously.

This is powered by Liquid Memory, a dynamic context caching system that allows the model to retain "working memory" of projects indefinitely, effectively removing the 10M token ceiling for active sessions.

Availability

Gemini 3.5 Pro is available starting today in Google AI Studio and will roll out to Gemini Advanced subscribers over the next week. We can't wait to see what you build with it.
